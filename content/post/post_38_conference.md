Dr. Hu attended the 1st [International Conference on Digital Intelligence for Energy Systems (ICDIES)](https://events.polyu.edu.hk/rise_digital_intelligence/home) in Hong Kong, 5-8 January 2025.

He was invited to deliver talks at two workshops: 'Workshop 1 - Data Science and Technology for Smart Buildings' and 'Workshop 6 - Artificial Intelligence for Energy Management'. He also chaired one session - System Performance Prediction and Analysis.
