Dr. Hu visited the Department of Building Science at Tsinghua University on 11 November. During the visit, he delivered a talk titled “Decarbonization of Building and Urban Energy Systems: A Multiscale Data-driven Approach,” sharing insights on how data-driven methods can support deep decarbonization across multiple spatial scales.

He also had engaging discussions with faculty members and students on topics such as UBEM, LLMs, Energy resilience, etc. 

Many thanks to Prof. Da Yan for the warm hospitality and meaningful exchanges.
